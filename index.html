Thars Crystals Up In Them Thar Hills!

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game (the "Target Score". There are four crystals below.
  2. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your "Total Score".
  3. You win the game by matching your "Total Score" to "Target Score", you lose the game if your "Total Score" goes above the random number ("Target Score").
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time when the game starts or restarts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
